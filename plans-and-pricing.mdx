Altarflow offers three flat-rate plans built around where your church is today and where it's headed. There are no per-user fees, no module add-ons, and no surprise charges — every plan includes unlimited admins, bilingual English/Spanish support, and free migration of up to five years of historical data. Most churches that patch together a giving platform, QuickBooks, and spreadsheets are already paying $120–$300 per month. Altarflow replaces that entire stack.

<Note>
  A **4% processing fee** applies to digital donations (Apple Pay, Google Pay, card, ACH). Cash and check donations tracked manually carry no processing fee. Cancel anytime — no long-term contracts.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="What's the difference between Seed and Growth?">
  Seed is for churches whose congregation gives in person — cash, check, or envelope — and don't need a digital payment processor yet. Growth adds digital giving (Apple Pay, Google Pay, card, ACH), daily Stripe payouts, and the full AI agent suite that categorizes expenses and matches Zelle and Venmo donations automatically.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I switch plans as my church grows?">
  Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from **Settings → Billing**. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you charge per user or per member?">
  No. All plans include unlimited admins and there are no per-member fees. You pay one flat monthly rate regardless of how many team members you invite or how large your congregation is.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does the free data migration work?">
  The Altarflow team imports your existing donor records, giving history, and expense data — up to five years back — at no charge. It works with exports from ChurchTrac, Breeze, Tithely, Planning Center, QuickBooks, and spreadsheets. Migration typically completes within your onboarding session.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
  Altarflow starts with a live 15-minute demo rather than a self-serve trial. The demo is a working session — you see the platform on your own data and leave with a clear picture of what changes. Book yours at altarflow.com/contact.
</Accordion>
