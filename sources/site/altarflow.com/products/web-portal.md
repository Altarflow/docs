MAKE IT YOURS

### Your Brand. Top to Bottom.

Custom fonts, text colors, button colors, background, logo, hero image — every pixel is yours. Add button animations. Arrange your links by section, then pick a layout: stacked, grid, or carousel. No templates that look like everyone else's.

ONE PLACE TO ENGAGE

### Every Link Your Community Needs.

Donation link, public forms, social media, and events with Google Maps and tap-to-add-to-calendar built in. Every link your church hands out — on one page you fully control.

![Every Link Your Community Needs.](https://altarflow.com/images/products/web-portal/one-place-to-engage.webp)

NFC TAP PROGRAMMING

### Every Tap, the Right Page.

Program each NFC tap to open a specific section of your portal — give on pews, sign up at the welcome desk, RSVP at the door. Place them anywhere in the building. Reassign any tap from your dashboard the moment plans change.

![Every Tap, the Right Page.](https://altarflow.com/images/products/web-portal/nfc-tap-programming.webp)
