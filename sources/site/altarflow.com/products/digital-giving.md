QUICK GIVE

### Tap. Give. Done.

Donors select an amount and fund, then tap Apple Pay or Google Pay. Under 5 seconds. No account needed for first-time donors — just tap and give.

![Tap. Give. Done.](https://altarflow.com/images/products/digital-giving/quick-give.webp)

DONOR PORTAL

### One Portal for Every Donor.

At donor.altarflow.com, donors see every donation to every Altarflow organization. Tax documents, stored payment methods, and giving with just a phone number. One identity across the entire network.

![One Portal for Every Donor.](https://altarflow.com/images/products/digital-giving/donor-portal.webp)

DAILY PAYOUTS

### Your Money, Next Day.

Daily payouts by default — not 7-14 day ACH transfers. See detailed breakdowns per payout: every transaction, donor, fees, and net received. Cash flow you can actually plan around.

![Your Money, Next Day.](https://altarflow.com/images/products/digital-giving/daily-payouts.webp)

NFC TAP

### Tap the Card. Give in Seconds.

Physical NFC tags placed on pews, welcome cards, and kiosks. Members tap their phone to open your giving page — every tag tracked, every destination reassignable from your dashboard. QR code backup for older phones.

![Tap the Card. Give in Seconds.](https://altarflow.com/images/products/digital-giving/nfc-tap.webp)
