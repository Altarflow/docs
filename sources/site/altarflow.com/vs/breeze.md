## What is Altarflow?

Altarflow is the financial operating system for churches. Donations, expenses, reporting, and digital giving in one platform — not five. From $99/month, replacing the giving-platform-plus-QuickBooks-plus-receipt-tool stack most churches patch together.

Breeze is a CRM with giving attached. Altarflow is a financial OS with a CRM attached. Different tools, different problems.

Who should pick what

Pick Breeze if your main need is tracking people, volunteers, and events — and you already have QuickBooks or a treasurer handling finance elsewhere. Pick Altarflow if finance is what's actually slowing you down.

Bottom line

Most Breeze churches also pay $19–$80/mo for QuickBooks or Aplos plus a receipt tool — a $120–180/mo stack. Altarflow replaces all three for $99/mo flat.
