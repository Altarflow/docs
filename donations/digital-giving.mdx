Altarflow's digital giving removes every obstacle between a generous impulse and your bank account. Donors don't need to download an app, create an account, or fumble with a website. They select an amount, choose a fund, and tap — done in under 5 seconds. You receive the money the next day, not 7–14 days later like most platforms. Everything is powered by Stripe and managed entirely from your Altarflow dashboard.

## Quick Give

Quick Give is the core donation flow every donor uses when giving digitally through Altarflow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Donor opens the giving page">
    Your church's giving page is accessible via a direct link, an NFC tap tag, or a QR code. No app download required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Donor selects an amount and fund">
    Preset amounts appear for one-tap selection, or the donor enters a custom amount. They choose which fund the gift goes to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Donor taps Apple Pay or Google Pay">
    The payment sheet appears instantly on the donor's phone. One biometric confirmation and the gift is sent. The entire flow takes under 5 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="First-time donors give without an account">
    New donors do not need to create an account to complete a gift. Altarflow captures the transaction and you can match it to a donor profile afterward — or let the AI do it automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Verified donors can store up to 3 cards in their Altarflow donor profile for even faster checkout on repeat visits.
</Note>

## NFC Tap Tags

NFC tap tags are physical cards or stickers you place anywhere in your church. When a member taps their phone to the tag, it opens your giving page instantly — no QR code scanner, no typing a URL.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Flexible Placement" icon="location-dot" href="/donations/digital-giving">
    Place NFC tags on pews, welcome cards, kiosks, offering stations, or anywhere your congregation naturally pauses during a service.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reassignable Destinations" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left" href="/donations/digital-giving">
    Each tag's destination URL is managed from your Altarflow dashboard. Redirect any tag to a campaign, a specific fund, or a seasonal giving page without replacing the physical tag.
  </Card>
  <Card title="QR Code Backup" icon="qrcode" href="/donations/digital-giving">
    Every NFC tag includes a QR code on the same card for donors using older phones that don't support NFC — nobody is left out.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Per-Tag Tracking" icon="chart-bar" href="/donations/digital-giving">
    Altarflow tracks which tag a donation came from, so you can see which placement locations drive the most giving.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Daily Payouts via Stripe

Most digital giving platforms hold your donations for 7–14 days before transferring them. Altarflow pays out daily by default.

Every business day, the previous day's donations are deposited directly into your church's bank account. You don't need to request a payout or change any settings — it happens automatically.

Each payout comes with a detailed breakdown you can review from your dashboard:

<Accordion title="What's included in a payout breakdown?">
  - A line-by-line list of every transaction included in the payout
  - The donor name and amount for each gift
  - The 4% processing fee deducted per transaction
  - The net amount deposited to your account
  - The payout date and reference ID for reconciliation
</Accordion>

This level of detail makes bank reconciliation straightforward: every deposit in your bank statement maps directly to a dated payout report in Altarflow.

## Processing Fee

Altarflow charges a **4% processing fee** on all digital donations. This fee is deducted before the payout is sent to your account and is itemized in every payout breakdown so you always know the exact net amount received.

<Tip>
Some churches choose to display an optional fee-coverage prompt in their Quick Give flow, giving donors the option to add 4% to their gift so the church receives the full intended amount. Contact your account team to enable this option.
</Tip>

## Donor Portal

Every donor who gives through any Altarflow-connected church automatically gets access to **donor.altarflow.com** — a self-service portal where they can:

- View their complete giving history across every Altarflow organization they've donated to
- Download their own year-end tax documents anytime, without contacting your church office
- Manage their stored payment methods (up to 3 cards)
- Make new donations using their phone number as their identity

The donor portal is included on the Growth and Scale plans and requires no setup from your side — donors access it automatically once they've made their first digital gift.
