Altarflow brings every donation your church receives — no matter how it arrives — into a single, clean view. Whether someone taps Apple Pay during the service, drops a check in the offering plate, or sends $50 over Zelle on a Tuesday, every gift lands in the same table with the donor's name, the fund it belongs to, and a complete audit trail. No spreadsheets. No missing rows. No end-of-year scrambles.

## All Sources, One Table

Every donation from every source appears in a single unified table. Each row shows the donor name, gift amount, processing fee (if any), payment method, fund, and status — all at a glance. You never have to reconcile between a digital giving report and a paper ledger again.

You can **edit any record inline** directly from the table. Fix a typo in a donor name, reassign a gift to a different fund, or update a gift amount without leaving the view. Changes are saved instantly and reflected in every connected report and tax statement.

## Manually Adding Donations

Not every gift flows in automatically. When you receive cash, a check, or an in-person envelope, you can add a donation record manually in seconds — just select the donor, enter the amount, choose the fund, and save. Manual donations are first-class citizens in Altarflow: they appear in the same table, count toward campaign totals, and are included in year-end tax statements.

## Fund Assignment Per Gift

Every donation in Altarflow is assigned to a fund — General, Building, Missions, or any fund you've created. You can set a default fund for your Quick Give flow and override it per-gift when needed. Fund-level totals roll up automatically into your financial reports so you always know exactly how much has come in for each purpose.

## Donor Matching

Altarflow maintains a profile for every donor in your system. When a digital payment comes in, it's matched to the donor automatically using the phone number or card on file. When you enter a manual donation, you search and select the donor from your CRM. For Zelle, Venmo, and Cash App transfers, the AI agent matches the transfer to the right donor based on name and giving history — and flags any unknown senders for your review. Every gift, however it arrived, ends up connected to the right person.
