## About Altarflow

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Altarflow?">
    Altarflow is the financial operating system built specifically for churches. It replaces the combination of spreadsheets, a church management system, and QuickBooks that most churches currently run — with one platform that handles donations, expenses, reporting, member data, forms, and digital giving.

    What makes Altarflow different from other church software is its AI agents. Agents do the back-office work your treasurer currently does by hand: categorizing expenses, matching Zelle and Venmo gifts to donors and funds, scanning receipts with OCR, and reconciling transactions. Most churches using Altarflow recover 20 or more hours per month that previously went to manual financial administration.

    Altarflow is built in English and Spanish natively — not as an add-on.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Altarflow a good fit for small churches?">
    Yes. Altarflow is designed to serve churches of every size, including small and newly planted congregations. The Seed plan starts at $49/month and includes the Member CRM, donation tracking, expense management, forms, and the web portal — everything a small church needs to run its finances cleanly.

    Small churches often benefit the most from Altarflow because they have the least administrative capacity. A bivocational pastor or a volunteer treasurer should not be spending Sunday evenings reconciling Zelle payments. Altarflow handles that automatically so the mission comes first.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Altarflow work in Spanish?">
    Yes. Altarflow is bilingual by default, not as a translation add-on. Every part of the platform — the dashboard, reports, forms, donor-facing giving flow, and member communications — is available in both English and Spanish.

    For reports and forms, you can toggle between English and Spanish output without rebuilding anything. For forms, you write both translations in a single editor and visitors choose their language at the top of the form. For reports, you switch languages before exporting to PDF or sharing a live link.

    This makes Altarflow a strong fit for Hispanic churches, bilingual congregations, and any church that serves a community where Spanish is the primary language.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What features does Altarflow include?">
    Altarflow includes:

    - **Donation tracking** — digital giving (Apple Pay, Google Pay, card, ACH), manual entry (cash, check, envelope), Zelle and Venmo AI matching, campaigns, and year-end tax statements
    - **Expense management** — manual entry, OCR receipt scanning, email receipt forwarding, bank and credit card connections, AI expense categorization, and fund allocation
    - **Financial reports** — donation reports, expense reports, and the financial analysis report (income vs. expenses, burn rate, net income growth, donation trends)
    - **Member CRM** — automatically built profiles, giving history, form submissions, attendance, custom fields, family groupings, and full-text search
    - **Form builder** — drag-and-drop, bilingual, six built-in templates, CRM auto-sync on submission
    - **Web portal** — fully branded church landing page with NFC tap programming, engagement analytics, QR codes, and OG card generation
    - **AI agents** — expense categorization, Zelle/Venmo matching, OCR receipt processing, and (on the Scale plan) custom sub-agents and WhatsApp notifications

    All plans include unlimited admins, bilingual support, and free data migration.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Altarflow different from ChurchTrac, Breeze, Tithely, or Planning Center?">
    ChurchTrac, Breeze, Tithely, and Planning Center are church management systems. They manage people, schedules, and communications well. Financial management is a secondary feature in each of them — most churches that use these tools also run a separate spreadsheet or QuickBooks to actually manage money.

    Altarflow is built finance-first. The core product is a financial operating system: donations, expenses, reporting, and the AI agents that automate the reconciliation work. People management (CRM, forms, portal) is built into the same platform so your giving records and your member records are always in sync — but the financial layer is the foundation, not an add-on.

    Specific differences:
    - **Zelle and Venmo matching** — Altarflow is the only church platform that auto-matches Zelle and Venmo payments to donors and funds using AI agents. Every other platform requires manual reconciliation.
    - **AI agents that do work** — Other platforms have added AI chatbots that answer questions. Altarflow's agents act: they categorize, match, reconcile, and learn.
    - **Bilingual-native** — Altarflow was built bilingual from day one. Spanish support in other platforms is typically limited or bolted on.
    - **Daily payouts** — Altarflow processes through Stripe with daily payouts. Most competing platforms settle on a weekly or longer cycle.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Setup & Migration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to set up Altarflow?">
    Most churches are live in under two hours. Setup typically goes: book a demo, Altarflow's team migrates your historical data, you connect your bank and configure your giving settings, and you hand out the QR code on Sunday.

    There is no lengthy implementation process, no paid consultant, and no technical configuration required on your part. Altarflow handles the migration and walks you through the setup on a call.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I migrate from ChurchTrac, Breeze, Tithely, or QuickBooks?">
    Altarflow's team handles the migration for you — it is not a self-service import. You export your data from your current system (donor records, giving history, member list), provide the files to the Altarflow team, and they load it into your account.

    Supported migration sources include ChurchTrac, Breeze, Tithely, Planning Center, QuickBooks, and Excel or CSV spreadsheets. Altarflow migrates up to five years of donor and donation history. Most migrations are completed the same day, and your historical data is available in your reports and CRM immediately after.

    Free migration is included on all plans — there is no additional charge.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What support is included?">
    All plans include standard customer support via email at [support@altarflow.com](mailto:support@altarflow.com). The Growth plan includes standard support with typical response times during business hours. The Scale plan includes **priority support** with faster response times and a **dedicated onboarding** session to get your team fully set up.

    During the current beta period, Altarflow's team is particularly hands-on with every customer. Beta customers can expect direct access to the team for questions, feedback, and issue resolution.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does Altarflow cost?">
    Altarflow has three flat monthly plans:

    - **Seed — $49/month** — For churches that give primarily by cash and check. Includes manual donation and expense tracking, OCR receipt scanning, Member CRM, forms, web portal, and bilingual support.
    - **Growth — $99/month** — The plan most churches use. Everything in Seed, plus digital giving (Apple Pay, Google Pay, card, ACH), daily Stripe payouts, AI expense categorization, AI Zelle and Venmo matching, donor portal, year-end tax statements, fund accounting, and board-ready financial reports.
    - **Scale — $149/month** — For ministries that need advanced automation. Everything in Growth, plus an AI budgeting agent, WhatsApp notifications, custom AI sub-agents, MCP integrations, multi-campus support, priority support, and dedicated onboarding.

    Digital donations processed through Altarflow carry a 4% processing fee. There are no per-user charges, no per-member charges, and no setup fees. All plans include unlimited admins and free data migration.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods does Altarflow accept?">
    Altarflow accepts major credit cards and ACH bank transfers for subscription billing. For digital donations processed through your church's giving flow, Altarflow supports Apple Pay, Google Pay, credit and debit cards, and ACH bank transfers. Giving payouts are deposited to your connected bank account via Stripe on a daily basis.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Altarflow does not currently offer a self-serve free trial. Instead, you start with a demo call where the team walks through Altarflow using your church's actual scenario — your current tools, your data, your questions. This gives you a much clearer picture of what Altarflow will look like for your church than a generic trial account would.

    To book a demo, visit [altarflow.com/contact](https://altarflow.com/contact).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change plans?">
    Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your billing settings. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you will be billed a prorated amount for the remainder of the current period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. There are no long-term contracts and no cancellation fees. You can cancel your Altarflow subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, you can export your data before your account closes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Altarflow offer nonprofit discounts?">
    Altarflow's pricing is already set to be accessible for small churches and nonprofits — starting at $49/month without any feature gating on core functionality. If your church is in an unusual financial situation (a new plant, a mission organization in an underserved community), reach out at [support@altarflow.com](mailto:support@altarflow.com) and the team will work with you.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security & Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my church's data secure?">
    Yes. Altarflow implements bank-level security practices across the platform:

    - **Encryption in transit and at rest** — all data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
    - **Secure payment processing** — all payment and financial data is handled by Stripe; Altarflow does not store credit card numbers or banking credentials
    - **Access controls** — role-based access controls ensure that team members only see the data their role requires
    - **Regular security assessments** — Altarflow conducts ongoing security reviews of its infrastructure

    Altarflow does not sell, rent, or share your church's data with third parties. For full details, see the [Privacy Policy](https://altarflow.com/privacy).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my data?">
    Yes. You own your data and can export it at any time. Donation records, member profiles, expense records, and financial reports are all exportable as CSV or PDF from your dashboard. If you ever leave Altarflow, you take your data with you — there is no lock-in and no export fee.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Beta Program

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What does 'beta' mean for Altarflow?">
    Altarflow is in active beta, which means the product is live, churches are using it every day, and real money is flowing through it — but the team is still building features and refining the experience based on customer feedback.

    For you as a customer, beta means two things: you get a lower price than the eventual general availability price, and you get direct access to the Altarflow team. Your feedback shapes what gets built next. Beta customers have influenced the product's bilingual features, the Zelle matching logic, and the financial analysis report.

    Beta does not mean unstable. Core financial features — giving, expense tracking, reporting — are production-ready and used by churches handling real transactions every week.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Altarflow support multi-campus churches?">
    Multi-campus and multi-site support is available on the Scale plan at $149/month. This includes separate fund accounting per campus, consolidated reporting across campuses, and the ability for each campus to operate with its own settings while sharing a parent account.

    If you lead a multi-campus ministry and want to understand how Altarflow would map to your structure, [book a demo](https://altarflow.com/contact) and the team will walk through your specific setup.
