Altarflow turns every transaction your church records into a ready-to-read report — no spreadsheets, no end-of-month scrambles, no accountant required. Whether you need a quick giving summary before Sunday's announcement or a board-ready financial package for your annual meeting, you can generate exactly what you need in under a minute.

## Types of reports

Altarflow gives you three core report types, each designed around a specific question your leadership team needs to answer.

**Donation reports** answer "Who gave, how much, and to which fund?" You see individual donor records, total giving by period, campaign performance, and method breakdowns (cash, check, digital, Zelle). Every donation your church has recorded flows into this report automatically.

**Expense reports** answer "Where did the money go?" Expenses are organized by category, fund, and time period so you can see spending patterns across programs — useful for compliance reviews, budget planning, and board presentations.

**Financial analysis** answers "How healthy is our church financially?" It combines income and expense data into a single view: income vs. expenses, burn rate, net income growth, donation trends, and operating costs. See the [Financial Analysis guide](/reports/financial-analysis) for a full walkthrough.

## How to generate a report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Reports section">
    From your Altarflow dashboard, navigate to **Reports** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a report type">
    Select **Donations**, **Expenses**, or **Financial Analysis** from the report type menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your date range">
    Choose a preset range (this month, this quarter, this year) or enter a custom start and end date. Altarflow stores up to five years of history, so you can pull any period you have data for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply optional filters">
    Narrow the report by **campaign**, **fund**, **expense category**, or **donor** using the filter panel. You can stack multiple filters — for example, all donations to the Building Fund in Q3.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and review">
    Click **Generate Report**. Your report appears immediately with totals, charts, and line-item detail. From here you can export or share it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key metrics at a glance

Every report surfaces the numbers your leadership actually needs:

| Metric | Where it appears |
|---|---|
| Total giving for period | Donation reports |
| Giving by fund / campaign | Donation reports |
| Donor count and average gift | Donation reports |
| Spending by category | Expense reports |
| Variance from prior period | Expense reports |
| Income vs. expenses | Financial analysis |
| Burn rate | Financial analysis |
| Net income growth | Financial analysis |
| Donation trend (month over month) | Financial analysis |
| Operating costs | Financial analysis |

## Export options

Once your report is ready, you have three ways to share it:

- **PDF** — formatted for print and board presentations, with your church name and date range in the header
- **CSV** — a raw data export for your accountant or bookkeeper to import into QuickBooks or Excel
- **Live link** — a shareable URL that always reflects the latest data; useful when you want leadership to check numbers without sending repeated exports

Every report is available in **English and Spanish**. Use the bilingual toggle to switch languages before exporting or sharing — no reformatting required.
