Altarflow is the financial operating system built specifically for churches. Instead of patching together a giving platform, a spreadsheet, a receipt inbox, and a separate accounting tool, Altarflow brings donations, expenses, reports, member CRM, forms, a web portal, and AI agents into one place — in both English and Spanish. Whether you're a solo bivocational pastor or a growing multi-campus ministry, Altarflow replaces the back-office burden with agents that do the work while you stay in control.

## The problems Altarflow solves

Most churches aren't behind because their people lack skill — they're behind because their tools were never built for church finance. These are the three patterns Altarflow eliminates:

<Accordion title="Zelle and payment-app chaos">
  Zelle, Venmo, and Cash App transfers hit the church bank account with names like "Unknown" or "Rosa M." — no fund, no record, no donor match. Altarflow's AI agents watch the bank feed, detect every payment-app transfer, create the donation record, and match it to the right donor and fund automatically. No other church platform does this.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Spreadsheet reconciliation graveyard">
  Donations_2026_FINAL_v3.xlsx with broken formulas, duplicate donor names, and a `#REF!` error in March. Churches on spreadsheets lose 20+ hours a month to manual reconciliation. Altarflow cuts that to under two hours — or often under 20 minutes — because the agents do the categorization, matching, and reconciliation for you.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Tools that don't talk to each other">
  Giving in a ChMS. Accounting in QuickBooks. Receipts in an email inbox. Reports assembled by hand every month. Altarflow replaces all three layers — tracking, visibility, and automation — in one platform, so your books are never somewhere else.
</Accordion>

## How the platform is organized

Altarflow is built around seven interconnected modules. Each one handles a distinct job, and together they cover every financial and administrative task your church faces:

| Module | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Donations** | Tracks every gift — digital, cash, check, Zelle, Venmo — by donor, fund, and campaign |
| **Expenses** | Captures receipts via OCR, email forwarding, or bank sync; AI categorizes everything |
| **Reports** | Generates board-ready donation, expense, and financial-analysis reports in seconds |
| **CRM** | Builds complete member profiles automatically from giving, forms, and attendance |
| **Forms** | Drag-and-drop builder for prayer requests, RSVPs, signups — bilingual, synced to CRM |
| **Web Portal** | A branded church page with giving, events, and NFC taps — no website needed |
| **AI Agents** | The engine across all modules: categorize, match, scan, and learn from corrections |
