## How profiles are created automatically

Altarflow creates and updates member profiles from several sources without any action required from your staff:

- **Digital giving** — When someone donates through your Altarflow giving link, a profile is created with their name, email, and giving record. Subsequent gifts update the same profile automatically.
- **Zelle matching** — Altarflow's AI agent matches incoming Zelle transfers to existing members by name and phone number. When a match is found, the gift is added to that member's giving history. When no match exists, a new profile is created with the available contact information.
- **Form submissions** — When someone submits any form built in Altarflow (a visitor card, prayer request, event RSVP, baptism application), the submission either creates a new profile or updates the existing one matched by email or phone. Form fields flow directly into CRM fields.

You can also add members manually at any time — useful for people who give only by cash or check and have never submitted a digital form.

## Custom fields

Every church tracks its people differently. Altarflow lets you define any custom field you need without touching a database or asking a developer. Custom fields appear on every member profile and are searchable.

**Available field types:**

| Type | Example uses |
|---|---|
| **Text** | Notes, hometown, emergency contact name |
| **Date** | Baptism date, membership date, follow-up date |
| **Dropdown** | Small group, campus location, language preference |
| **Boolean (yes/no)** | Background check completed, active volunteer, first-generation member |

**Common custom fields churches create:**

- **Baptism date** — Track when each member was baptized; use it to recognize anniversaries
- **Small group** — Assign members to their group for easy filtering and communication
- **Language preference** — Tag English, Spanish, or bilingual preference for targeted outreach
- **Ministry team** — Note which team(s) a member serves on (worship, children's ministry, ushers, etc.)

Custom fields are visible on the profile page and searchable from the main member search. You can add, rename, or remove fields at any time.

## Family and group linking

Families are the natural unit of church life, and Altarflow reflects that. You can link any members into a family unit. Once linked:

- Giving statistics roll up to the family level — useful for year-end giving statements and capital campaign reporting
- Communications show correct household addressing
- You can view the full family on one screen without switching between individual profiles

Beyond families, you can create custom groups for any organizational unit: a small group, a volunteer team, a youth cohort, a board committee. Groups work the same way as families but without the implied relationship type. You can add or remove members from any group at any time.

## Full-text search

Altarflow's member search runs across every field on every profile simultaneously. Type a name, phone number, email address, or even a custom field value and results appear in under a second.

Examples of what you can find instantly:

- The Sanchez family's most recent donation amount
- Every member with "nursery" as their ministry team
- All members who submitted a baptism application in the last six months
- A member whose name you only partially remember — partial-name search works

Search results link directly to the full profile, so you are never more than one click from the information you need.
