Altarflow's form builder lets you create any form your church needs in minutes — no code, no plugins, no third-party tools required. Every submission automatically creates or updates a member profile in your CRM, so nothing gets lost between a paper card and your records. And for bilingual churches, you build English and Spanish in one editor and let visitors choose their language at the top.

<Tip>
  Start from one of Altarflow's built-in templates instead of from scratch. Templates are pre-built for the most common church use cases and take less than five minutes to customize. You can always add, remove, or rearrange fields after choosing a template.
</Tip>

## Drag-and-drop builder — no code required

The form builder works entirely in your browser with a drag-and-drop interface. You add fields from a panel on the left and arrange them on the canvas by dragging. Each field has settings for label, placeholder text, required vs. optional, and help text. You can preview the form on a mobile screen at any time to make sure it looks right for members who will fill it out on their phones.

**Available field types:**

| Field type | Use it for |
|---|---|
| **Text (short)** | Name, city, emergency contact |
| **Text (long / paragraph)** | Prayer request detail, notes, testimony |
| **Email** | Contact email, parent email |
| **Phone** | Mobile number, home number |
| **Dropdown** | Fund selection, small group, campus |
| **Date** | Birthday, event date, baptism date |
| **Signature** | Volunteer agreements, commitment cards |
| **File upload** | ID documents, photos, supporting materials |
| **Conditional logic** | Show or hide fields based on a previous answer |

Conditional logic is particularly powerful for multi-purpose forms. For example, a single visitor form can show different follow-up questions depending on whether the visitor checks "first time" or "returning visitor."

## CRM auto-sync on submission

Every form submission is matched to your CRM automatically:

- If the email address or phone number on the submission matches an existing member profile, Altarflow updates that profile with the new information and logs the submission in the member's history.
- If no match is found, Altarflow creates a new member profile with the submitted contact details.

This means your CRM stays current without any CSV imports, copy-pasting, or manual data entry. A visitor who fills out your new-visitor form on Sunday morning has a complete profile in your CRM before the service ends.

Form fields map directly to CRM fields. If you add a "Preferred language" dropdown to a form, the selected value appears on the member's profile under that field. Custom fields you have created in the CRM are available to add to any form.

## Bilingual forms: English and Spanish in one editor

For bilingual churches, Altarflow's form builder lets you write English and Spanish translations side by side in a single editor. You do not manage two separate forms or keep them in sync manually — every field has an English label and a Spanish label, and the translation is stored together.

When a visitor opens the form, they see a language toggle at the top. They pick their language and fill out the form in that language. The submission comes into your CRM the same way regardless of which language the visitor chose — staff see the data in a consistent format.

This approach replaces the common workaround of maintaining two forms (one in English, one in Spanish) and reconciling submissions from both. For the more than 40,000 Hispanic and bilingual churches in the United States, this is a meaningful operational improvement.

## Sharing and embedding forms

Once you publish a form, you have several ways to get it in front of your community:

- **Direct link** — Copy the form URL and share it in a text message, email, or WhatsApp group
- **QR code** — Altarflow generates a QR code for every form; download it and print it in the bulletin, on a welcome card, or on a poster at the welcome desk
- **Web portal** — Add the form as a link on your Altarflow web portal so anyone who visits your church's page can access it
- **Embed code** — If your church has an existing website, embed the form using the provided code snippet; it functions the same way and submissions still sync to your CRM

Forms are mobile-friendly by default and have been tested on iOS and Android browsers. No app download is required for visitors to fill them out.
