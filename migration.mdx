## What can be migrated

Altarflow migrates the records that matter most to your church's financial continuity:

- **Donor history** — every donor's contact information and full giving record, up to five years back
- **Donation records** — individual gift records including date, amount, method, fund, and campaign
- **Member data** — member profiles including names, contact information, and any additional data your current system stores in exportable fields

Five years of history means your year-end tax statements, your capital campaign records, and your long-term giving trends all move with you. Your donors do not experience a gap in their giving history, and your leadership can pull historical reports from day one.

## How the migration works

Migration is handled by Altarflow's team — it is not a self-service import tool you figure out on your own.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Book your demo">
    Schedule a call at [altarflow.com/contact](https://altarflow.com/contact). During the call, the team will confirm your migration source and walk you through exactly what to export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export your data from your current system">
    Follow the export instructions for your current platform (see below for what to prepare). Most exports take five to fifteen minutes. You do not need to clean or reformat the data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your files to the Altarflow team">
    Share your export files securely with the team. They will review the data, map fields to Altarflow's structure, and load it into your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    The team will walk you through a review of the migrated data — donor count, donation totals, date range — so you can confirm everything came across correctly before going live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Once you confirm the migration, your account is active. Most churches complete this entire process in under two hours and are ready to give and track donations the same day.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to prepare before migration

Getting your data export ready before your demo call makes the process faster. Here is what to gather:

**From your current ChMS (ChurchTrac, Breeze, Tithely, Planning Center):**
- Donor / member export (all contact records)
- Giving history export — select the maximum date range your system allows, up to five years
- Fund or campaign list if your system tracks designated giving separately

**From QuickBooks:**
- Customer / donor list export
- Transaction detail report covering your giving history
- Chart of accounts export for expense category mapping

**From spreadsheets:**
- Your donation records spreadsheet — include donor name, date, amount, and fund or category columns
- Your member or contact list

You do not need to reconcile duplicates, standardize formats, or do any data cleanup. The Altarflow team will handle deduplication and field mapping during the migration.

## What happens to your data after migration

Once your data is in Altarflow:

- **It is yours.** You can export your data at any time as CSV or PDF directly from your dashboard — no request needed and no fee.
- **It is complete.** Migrated donation records appear in your reports, donor profiles, and giving statements immediately. There is no waiting period.
- **Your current system stays intact.** Migration does not delete or alter anything in your old system. You can run both in parallel until you are confident in the switch, and then cancel your old subscription when you are ready.

If you ever leave Altarflow, the same applies: you export your data, and you take it with you. There is no lock-in and no exit fee.
