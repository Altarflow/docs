Altarflow is the financial operating system built for churches. It brings together donations, expenses, reports, and member management in one bilingual platform — with AI agents that do the back-office work your treasurer does by hand today. Whether you're receiving Zelle transfers, processing digital gifts, or reconciling monthly books, Altarflow handles the details so you can lead.

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Book a demo or start your account">
    [Schedule a 15-minute demo](https://altarflow.com/contact) and walk through Altarflow with your real numbers. Your account is provisioned before the call ends.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Migrate your existing data">
    Altarflow migrates up to five years of donor history from ChurchTrac, Breeze, Tithely, or QuickBooks — free, in under two hours. See the [Migration guide](/migration).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your bank and set up giving">
    Link your bank accounts and credit cards so AI agents can start categorizing transactions. Activate Quick Give so your congregation can donate on Sunday. See the [Quickstart](/quickstart).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team and approve">
    Add your treasurer, bookkeeper, or finance volunteers as admins. Review what the AI has done, correct anything once, and approve — the rest takes minutes, not days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Altarflow is fully bilingual in English and Spanish. Every feature, report, and member-facing form works in both languages out of the box.
</Note>
